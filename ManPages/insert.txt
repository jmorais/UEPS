
Inserts a new process on scheduler.

Syntax: insert <processName> <time> <memory>, where:

<processName>: the new process' name.
<time>: the time the process needs to work.
<memory>: the memory the process needs to work.

E.g.:
  $> insert p1 10 64
  Process p1 created successfully.

  $> insert p2 30 64
  Process p2 created successfully.
